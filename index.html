Welcome to our website

Adama Spinning Factory is a private company established in 2008 G.C under Adama Development PLC to produce 100% cotton yarn and socks products with a state of the art technology. It has a young & dedicated employees and visionary management. So far, it has created a direct job opportunity for about 450 employees. The company has planned to undertake a forward and backward integration project to be realized in the near future.
